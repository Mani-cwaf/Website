    What is AI?

    AI stands for Artificial intelligence Often what people consider AI, is one component of AI, machine learning. AI requires specialized hardware and software for writing and training machine learning algorithms. Multiple programming languages are used for AI, but Python and Java are popular choices.

    How does AI work?

    Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems using machine learning technology.
