    What Is Artificial Intelligence?

  • The term artificial intelligence may be used for any computer that has characteristics similar to thehuman brain,including the ability to think critically, make decisions, and increase productivity. The foundation of AI ishuman insights that may be determined in such a manner that machines can easily realize the jobs, from themost simple to the most complicated.
  • Insights that are synthesized are the result of intellectual activity, including study, analysis, logic,and observation. Tasks, including robotics, control mechanisms, computer vision, scheduling, and data mining,fall under the umbrella of artificial intelligence.
